Fyre is a documentary unveiling the story behind the catastrophe that was The Fyre Festival. In 2017 con-artist Billy McFarland and rapper Ja Rule aimed to stage the coolest party of a generation. An exclusive guest list of influencers and celebrities were invited to an island formally owned by drugs baron Pablo Escobar. As the date drew nearer, the nightmare began to unfold. Watch through your fingers as the whole enterprise collapses. Corruption, fraud and outright lies come together to give the guests a night they would never forget, for all the wrong reasons.
