## About

Movies and events every morning and night at Electromagnetic Field. Find us in Stage C.

## Morning Films

For the first time we are showing films in the morning for our younger attendees (although everyone is welcome).
Check the schedule for events startiong around 08:00 from Friday onwards. These showings will not feature ads and
limited snacks will be available.

For the first time, some of our features will be shown on original 35mm film. See how films _should_ be shown. :)

## Special Events

Watch out for appearances from directors and producers throughout the weekend.
