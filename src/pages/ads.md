## We Want To Advertise Your Cool Thing!

Would you like to see your name in lights? Finally get your 15 minutes of
fame? Cause confusion at next year's Canne Film Festival and a definate win at the Oscars? Here isn't your chance!

We have space for adverts before each film we show at Electromagnetic Field.
Grab your phone/camera/panaflex and make us an ad about your really cool
talk/installation/alluring hairdo. It doesn't matter what (or why) just so
long as it's entertaining and/or informative. Boast about your brilliance!

All we need is a maximum 30 second video file in any common format. 16:9
ratio with a maximum resolution of
1920 x 1080p. And let's keep it family-friendly folks. (That doesn't mean you
can't put in a bit of politics or have an opinion, just make it something you would show a friend's grandmother.)

Send your amazing creations to <a href="mailto:films@emfcamp.org">films@emfcamp.org</a> and maybe you'll be on the
silver screen at last!
